High Protein Meal Prep Chicken & Rice Bowls

High Protein Meal Prep Chicken & Rice Bowls

Prep: 15 min Cook: 25 min Total: 40 min Yield: 5 containers 420 cal · 42g protein

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless chicken breast
  • 2 cups jasmine or basmati rice, uncooked
  • 1.5 lbs broccoli florets
  • 3 tbsp olive oil, divided
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt, pepper, sesame seeds to taste

Instructions

  1. 1. Cook the rice

    Cook rice according to package instructions while you prep the chicken and broccoli.

  2. 2. Marinate the chicken

    Whisk 2 tbsp olive oil, soy sauce, honey, and garlic in a bowl. Add chicken breasts and let sit for 10 minutes.

  3. 3. Cook the chicken

    Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken 6-7 minutes per side until internal temperature reaches 165°F. Rest 5 minutes, then slice.

  4. 4. Roast the broccoli

    Toss broccoli with remaining olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast at 425°F for 15 minutes, or sauté in a hot pan for 5-6 minutes.

  5. 5. Assemble containers

    Divide rice, chicken, and broccoli evenly between 5 containers. Sprinkle with sesame seeds. Cool completely before sealing and refrigerating.

Tips & Common Questions

How long do these meal prep bowls last in the fridge?

Up to 5 days when cooled completely before sealing and stored in an airtight container. Reheat only the portion you're eating.

Can I freeze these meal prep bowls?

Yes, up to 3 months. Rice and chicken freeze well; broccoli texture softens slightly on reheat but stays edible.

Why does my chicken turn out dry when reheated?

Slicing the chicken before storing (rather than reheating a whole breast) and reheating covered with a splash of water helps retain moisture — dry reheats are usually from uncovered microwaving on high power.

Can I swap the rice for something lower carb?

Yes — cauliflower rice or quinoa both work as direct swaps without changing the chicken or broccoli steps.
