Ground Turkey and Sweet Potato Bowls

Prep: 10 min Cook: 25 min Total: 35 min Yield: 5 containers 380 cal · 34g protein

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground turkey (93% lean)
  • 2 large sweet potatoes, cubed
  • 1 lb green beans, trimmed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil, divided
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. 1. Roast the sweet potato

    Toss cubed sweet potato with 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast at 425°F for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway, until fork-tender.

  2. 2. Cook the turkey

    Heat remaining olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add ground turkey, smoked paprika, garlic powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Cook 8-10 minutes, breaking it up as it browns, until fully cooked through.

  3. 3. Steam the green beans

    Steam or blanch green beans for 4-5 minutes until bright green and just tender.

  4. 4. Assemble containers

    Divide turkey, sweet potato, and green beans evenly between 5 containers. Cool completely before sealing and refrigerating.

Tips & Common Questions

Can I use ground chicken instead of turkey?

Yes, ground chicken works as a direct 1:1 swap with the same cook time and seasoning.

How long do these bowls keep in the fridge?

Up to 5 days when cooled completely before sealing. Reheat only the portion you're eating.

Why does my ground turkey turn out dry?

Overcooking past fully-browned is the main cause — 93% lean turkey has less fat than beef, so pull it off the heat as soon as no pink remains rather than cooking further for extra browning.

Can I swap the sweet potato for regular potato?

Yes, though cook time may vary slightly — check for fork-tenderness rather than relying on the exact time listed.
